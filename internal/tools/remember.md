Record a durable fact about this environment as a shared memory in the brain, so
it primes every future session — yours and your teammates'. Memory is
multiplayer: when the brain is a shared Notion workspace, what you remember is
what the whole team starts their next hunt knowing, stamped with who learned it.

Use it when a hunt teaches you standing context worth keeping: where a log source
lives, a known-good baseline ("svc-deploy rotates keys nightly ~02:00 UTC"), a
detection or asset naming convention, a crown-jewel system, or a confirmed
environment quirk that explains what "normal" looks like.

Do NOT use it for hunt evidence (use record_finding) or threat intelligence —
indicators, TTPs, actors — (use record_intel); those are the hunt's output.
remember is only for durable background about the environment itself.

Keep each fact a single, specific sentence. Never store secrets or credentials.
